About Us

The Botswana Youth Sports Foundation aims to provide opportunities for young people to engage in sports and physical activities.

Our Mission:

To empower youth through sports, promote teamwork, and enhance physical fitness.

Our Vision:

A healthy and active youth community in Botswana.

Programs Offered:

Program Age Group Frequency
Soccer Training 8-18 Weekly
Basketball Clinics 10-18 Bi-weekly
Health Workshops All Ages Monthly
